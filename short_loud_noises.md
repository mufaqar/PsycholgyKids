🌿 SCENE 1 — The Innocent Morning
🎨 Image Prompt (~200 words)
A cozy kitchen in warm Studio Ghibli style during soft morning light. Golden sunlight streams through lace curtains, illuminating floating dust particles. A faceless child around five years old sits at a wooden table with a bowl of cereal, posture relaxed, feet swinging gently. The room feels peaceful—warm wooden tones, a small potted plant by the window, birds singing faintly outside. Suddenly, the mother reaches for the blender on the counter. The child's body language shifts instantly—shoulders lifting, hands gripping the table edge, eyes widening through head orientation. The blender cord being plugged in becomes the emotional trigger. The kitchen remains warm and beautiful, but the child's nervous system has already begun preparing for danger before any sound occurs. Soft textures, delicate sunlight, and the contrast between domestic peace and anticipatory fear create emotional depth.
