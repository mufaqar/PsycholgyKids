Why Your Child Fears the Dark — It's Not What You Think The Real Reason Darkness Terrifies Some Children (It's Not Monsters) When the Lights Go Out — What's Actually Scaring Your Child

📝 Short Description

Fear of the dark isn't about monsters under the bed — it's about what happens inside a child's brain when visual information disappears. Here's the psychology behind why darkness feels genuinely terrifying to some children.
